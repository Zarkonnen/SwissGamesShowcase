Senalux

Senalux is a hex-based puzzle game about optics where you solve challenging puzzles with colorful lasers. Use the red, green and blue lasers, combine them to the right color and illuminate the target. Senalux comes with many different optical components and over 200 levels as well as a level editor, so you can make your own puzzles and share them with others.

Developer: Pixel with Hat

Publisher: Pixel with Hat

Release Date: 2017-10-16
