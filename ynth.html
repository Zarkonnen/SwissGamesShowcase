Ynth

On a family trip, small red bug Kribl got hit by a vicious wind gust and was carried far away to a spooky marshland. Centuries ago mysterious creatures lived here in their bizarre labyrinth-like dwellings. To repel burglars, many deadly traps were installed. Looking for an exit, Kribl is straying through this ghost town on the marsh... Ynth is a clever new jump & turn game for iPhone and iPod Touch. It combines puzzle and 2d jump & run elements to a fascinating game that is easy to pick up and stays entertaining and challenging for hours.

Developer: krabl.com

Release Date: 2009
