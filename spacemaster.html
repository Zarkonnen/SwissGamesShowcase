spaceMaster

Fly your rocket through space and collect the coins which make you rich. But pay attention: space is full of dangerous comets and UFOs, which steel your coins and want to destroy you, and you can not slow down. Luckily, you can shoot your enemies with your lasergun. Have fun beating your highscore and compare it with your friends, thanks to Swarm! Move the spaceMasterShip when you tilt your Phone left and right. Acceleration is automatic and unfortunately, your break broke down!

Developer: Bernhard Webstudio

Release Date: 2015

Platforms: Java, Web, iOS, Android
