Nanorunners

nanorunners is a multiplayer race for up to 200 participants. all players steer avatar runners with their own smartphones. a simple and short game, but lots of fun. and the audience turns into an interacting entity.

Developer: and-or

Release Date: 2012

Platforms: big screen [mac + smartphones]

Awards: SGDA Swiss Game Developers Association, Swiss Game Award 2013. Finalist
