Dust Squad

DUST SQUAD - Tanks for Nothing is a puzzle action game for 1-6 players. You are the DUST SQUAD, a special unit of sentient tanks sent to explore and conquer a strange planet. Battle your way through levels filled with evil robot monsters eager to slice you and your friends apart. Evade destructive guard turrets while you’re waiting for your teammates to finally open that door. Find the glowing cores to upgrade your tank’s armor, engine and weapons.

Developer: AirConsole
