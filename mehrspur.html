Mehrspur

Beat the music. Play the music. Be the music. Mehrspur is an interactive music app - easy to start. Simply run over 3 colors in a chain to activate a music track. An activated track will play for 6 bars before fading out again. Try to make as many variations as possible!

Developer: kruz + kiyak

Release Date: 2011

Platforms: iPhone
