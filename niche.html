Niche - a genetics survival game

Niche is a digital board game about genetics, heredity and evolution.Trying to prevent an animal tribe from extinction is a tough task. A long journey through different landscapes awaits you!

Developer: Stray Fawn Studio

Release Date: 2017-09-21

Early Access Date: 2016-09-15

Platforms: PC, Mac, Linux

Awards: Winner Pro Helvetia Swiss Games, Winner (Silver) Quo Vadis - Indie Pitch, Official Selection Indie Prize, Finalist BIG Fest, Official Selection A MAZE Joburg
