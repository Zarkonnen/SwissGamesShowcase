Draw Me the Way!

Draw me the way is a puzzle game for Samsung S-Pen devices. It tells the story of how a noble knight called Sir Hainrich met a powerful wizard and how they embarked on a great journey searching for gold and glory. The player leads Sir Hainrich through dungeons by drawing arrows on the floor and thus drawing him the way.

Developer: 416Games

Release Date: 2013

Platforms: Samsung Store
