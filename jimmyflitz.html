Jimmy Flitz

«Once the cat runs out of the house, a cheeky little mouse wears her hat and blouse!» Swiss children laugh with the singing mouse Jimmy Flitz from the books and audiobooks by Roland Zoss. In this app, youngsters can learn to put on the right clothes for the season with Jimmy before going out to explore the world.

Developer: Roland Zoss

Release Date: 2012-04-21
