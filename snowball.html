SNOWBALL

SNOWBALL is a FPS action game with a Low-Poly style. This is the second game developped by PROTOKOLL Studio. Your goal is to protect a car in the snow from the enemies, the znowman. With your snowgun, you will be able to shoot on the enemies from 30 different waves. To help you, you will be able to pick an item like a fondue to regain your health or a gift to transform your snowgun in a minigun.

Developer: PROTOKOLL Studio

Release Date: 2019-01-16

Platforms: PC
