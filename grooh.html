Grooh!!

Grooh is a cute (and lazy?) monster that loves pineapples. Today, Grooh decides to explore the abandoned "Feilong Castle". There's a huge dragon printed on the entrance door!! But most importantly, rumors say that there's a secret treasure hidden there, and *who knows*, maybe it's full of pineapples? That's why YOU, oh mighty player, should help Grooh to find the treasure!

Developer: digiDingo

Release Date: 2012
