XV-1900/86

XV-1900/86 is a single-player story driven game inspired by the real events that took place during the Cold War. You control the thoughts and the actions of Barbara Krausse, as she is interrogated by the Stasi, the secret state police. The reason for her arrest remains unknown to Barbara and to you. As the player, you must try and figure out the reason while constantly fighting the temptation to give in or stand against the system!

Developer: Pulkit Singh

Early Access Date: 2020-06-19

Platforms: PC, Mac
