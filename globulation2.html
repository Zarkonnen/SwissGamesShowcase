Globulation 2

Globulation 2 is an innovative Real-Time Strategy (RTS) game which reduces micro-management by automatically assigning tasks to units.

Developer: originaly by Stéphane Magnenat and Luc-Olivier de Charrière, then developed as open-source by a team online

Early Access Date: 2000

Platforms: Linux, Windows, macOS
