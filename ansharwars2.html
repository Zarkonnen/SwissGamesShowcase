Anshar Wars 2

A Solid and Enjoyable Space Combat Experience Anshar Wars 2 is a comprehensive package attuned to the demands of a modern gaming audience which represents the pinnacle of phone-powered VR technology. It combines 360-degree movement, multiplayer, voice chat and the Oculus-powered ability to record screenshots and gameplay while playing. A must-see experience thanks to the already beloved gameplay mechanics, beautiful vivid, high-resolution graphics and a vastly improved gameplay experience.

Developer: OZWE Games, Sunnyside Games

Release Date: 2015-12-03

Platforms: Gear VR, Oculus Rift

Awards: Best VR Game Indie Prize 2017
