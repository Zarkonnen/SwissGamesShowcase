Ravage

Ravage, the latest game from Alphahelix, was released on September 1st, 1996. It features 9 levels (2 levels in the shareware release) Multiple plane scrolling Stunning explosions Intelligent enemies Great graphics f/x Realistic sound effects and a dance music inspired soundtrack also included is a 2 player/1 PC game mode where you and your buddy both fight against evil enemies on the same screen. Furthermore, you can upgrade your battleship in a shop with weapons and other goodies.

Developer: Alpha Helix

Release Date: 1996-09-01

Platforms: DOS
