OKO

OKO is a puzzle video game. OKO invites you on a hypnotic and mesmerizing journey around the planet, traveling through satellite images taken from the NASA database, and rich otherworldly soundscapes composed from recordings taken aboard space shuttles. Come float in space, and play with the earth from amongst the stars.
