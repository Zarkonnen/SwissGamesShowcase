Atomik Chaotik Bombing of Doom

In this collaborative game, each player steers one of the two planes carrying a large bomb meant to dismantle a nuclear power plant. The two players have to work together in order to transport the bomb to the target. But careful! The bomb is heavy, don't make the planes touch, and don't collide with the enemies, of which there are many!
