Rockstar Rampage

«Rockstar Rampage» is about a rockstar on world tour wrecking hotel rooms. Some rooms are shabby, some are classy - wreck them all! Do it with style and in rockstar-fashion and make it look good for the lurking paparazzi. Be on the front page of the daily newspaper - to become truly famous!
