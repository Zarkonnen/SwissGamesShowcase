Boxing Cats

Collect, evolve and fuse boxing cat cards. Discover more than 100 cute, mean, proud, cocky, crazy, angry, thrashed, flirty boxing cats and boxing cat managers. Set up boxing fights in your neighbourhood's venues and unlock more venues on the map. Become the greatest boxing cat manager of all time. Build boxing cat card decks to attack other managers and to fend off their attacks. Climb up the monthly boxing cat managers' rankings.
