Review Speedy Blupi

Help an egg find all the treasures
Author: Evgenii Kostrov
Date: 30 Apr 2017, tested on Nokia Lumia 920
8.3Excellent
Funny sounds, decent graphics, every level brings something new, interesting game play, several variants of controls
Paid continuation
Speedy Blupi represents a very amusing platformer for Windows Phone where you need to help the main character search for treasures. You are to manage a character looking like an egg, and at that he is not afraid of collisions with hard objects or falling from a great height. Obstacles and dangers are very common in the game and their number increases with each level.
