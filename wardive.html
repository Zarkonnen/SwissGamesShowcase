Wardive

wardive is an adaptive game with locative levels. it converts wlan-waves into game objects and levels. it reads the names of the hotspots in your immediate area and turns them into enemies, who try to take over your wardive-crystal. shield up! and try to fend them off with your touch finger! walk through e-toxic streets in your area, generated by hotspots! defeat hotspots & wlans! generate your own street & neighborhood-levels

Developer: and-or

Release Date: 2011

Platforms: Android
