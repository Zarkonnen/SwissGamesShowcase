Silhōuette

Silhōuette is a relaxing puzzle game about shapes and physics. Drop small objects onto an invisible large one and try to figure out which Silhōuette is hidden by studying the interactions. You can play through 60 predefined levels or try to beat the high score in Time Trial mode. A fun little game for the small breaks that life allows us during the day.

Developer: Gango Games

Release Date: 2016

Platforms: iOS, Android
