Cemetery Rush

Monsters escaped from the great mage Malthendor’s cemetery ! Help him to send them back by swiping them into their cage, but be careful, some monsters don’t get along well and Malthendor will get mad at you if you make too many mistakes ! Cemetery Rush is a fast paced, simple but addictive game ! Will you be able to memorize all the combinations ?

Developer: Alpagames

Release Date: 2015

Platforms: iOS, Android, Windows Store
