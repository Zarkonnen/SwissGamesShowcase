Sprout - find your way

Sprout - find your way is a story adventure in which the player controls a child who is left by his mother. Driven by the question why one is left behind, the player must follow the mother. Through small interactions, the player can solve puzzles and overcome obstacles while remaining focused on the atmosphere of the world.

Developer: Benjamin Gilli, Chris Leisi

Release Date: 2018

Platforms: PC, Mac
