Quiz and Fly

Quiz and Fly is a free serious game in the field of edutainment and financial literacy, suitable for a broad audience, young to old, French, Italian, German and English speaking. Players travel the globe to unlock new quizzes. The questions and answers are designed in the style of infographics. The app is provided for free by UBS Switzerland.

Developer: Gbanga

Sponsor: UBS

Release Date: 2014

Platforms: iOS, Android
