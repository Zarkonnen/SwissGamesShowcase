To Be Headed Or Not To Be

“To Be Headed Or Not to Be” is a cooperative only escape-room game. You incarnate a resurrected skeleton awakened by a failed experiment and you are not alone. Collaborate to overcome obstacles and escape the mansion to gain your freedom for this second life, or be forever kept as an experimental guinea pig.

Developer: Fruits of Yggdrasil

Release Date: 2019-11-22

Platforms: PC
