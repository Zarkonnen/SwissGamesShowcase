Anshar Wars

Anshar Wars is one of the first games available when the Gear VR is first released in December 2014 and the first multiplayer game ever on this platform. With the Gear VR Innovator Edition, Samsung puts the device in the hands of OZWE — amongst others — to craft amazing immersive experiences. The Innovator Edition is launched with Anshar Wars and VRtillery, along with a collection of high-quality sample experiences that show off what Gear VR is capable of.

Developer: OZWE Games

Release Date: 2014-12-01

Platforms: Gear VR
