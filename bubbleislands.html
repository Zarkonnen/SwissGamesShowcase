Bubble Islands

Bubble Islands is an exciting puzzle and arcade game featuring 10 different and cute characters. Challenge your opponents, connect your bubbles and preserve your island through series of funny but competitive duels!! Bubble Islands was finalist at the Independent Games Festival (IGF) in 2007. This annual event takes place during the Game Developer Conference (GDC), San Francisco.

Developer: Dot.invasion

Release Date: 2006

Platforms: Flash
