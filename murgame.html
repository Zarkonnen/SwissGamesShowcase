MurGame

A playful debris flow simulation, where players defend a quaint alpine village from giant mudslides. The game is aimed at players with a love for realistic data and behaviour, which is achieved through the game’s implementation of real-life debris flow data (RAMMS). It's also used for educational purposes, to raise awareness of the costs and dangers of giant mudslides in Switzerland and what kind of countermeasures can be made in high-risk locations.

Developer: Koboldgames

Sponsor: Geo7, SLF, Die Mobiliar, BAFU, Präventionsstiftung Kantonaler Gebäudeversicherungen
