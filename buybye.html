Buy! Bye!

BUY!BYE! is a multiplayer game for the Apple iPhone / iPad, which shows a normal day in the life of a broker in a humoristic manner.    The main goal of the game is to gather the majority of valuable stocks as fast as possible. You can play the game by touching the iPhone / iPad with either one or two fingers at the right time. The graphic style of the game is retro, with old school pixel graphics and sound. The game was developed by two Students of the Zurich University of the Arts in Switzerland.

Developer: Marc Gruber, Stefan Spieler

Release Date: 2011

Platforms: iPhone
