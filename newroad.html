New Road

It is like drawing. Except that what you draw, turns into a road. In “New Road” creating a 3D racing track is as simple as drawing some curves on your screen. But as soon as your engine starts, the actual challenge begins: try to reach the end of your track without crashing into anything. Not easy, as digital glitches are littered all over the road.

Developer: Christian Schnellmann

Release Date: 2013

Platforms: iOS
