Airport Radar

Playing as an air traffic controller you can visit and operate in a variety of famous airports worldwide, but your thirst for efficiency and safety is paramount to your success. A pitstop in Dubai will almost certainly lead to a timed level in Japan. From O’Hare International Airport to Heathrow, players must balance incoming planes to a safe landing as other aircraft stack up. Though it looks like the simplest task ever, ask any air traffic controller - it’s one of the hardest jobs in the world! The key skill is mental fortitude: if you let flight safety go to your head you might lose your landing streak.
