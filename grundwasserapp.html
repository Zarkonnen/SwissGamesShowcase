Grundwasser App

Learning app about groundwater with a laboratory you can play around with. In three short chapters, you can teach yourself how groundwater is formed, how we humans use it and what rivers have to do with it. Afterwards, you can play around in the groundwater laboratory and experience the interactions between groundwater, rain, and rivers.
